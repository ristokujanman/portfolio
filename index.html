About

Risto Kujanpää is a designer and a researcher.

His work oscillates between poles such as form–emptiness, language–nonrepresentational and sound–silence.
Working methods include, but are not limited to, collaboration–solitude, intuition–rationale and precision–big picture.

Risto's academic background lies in graphic design, science and urbanism.
His underground background is situated in soundscapes, electronic music and contemporary soundsystem culture.
